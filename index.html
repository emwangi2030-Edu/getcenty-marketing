How it works

From request to recipient in minutes

Structured approvals and real-time M-Pesa disbursements — with a permanent audit trail on every payment.

01

Create a payment request

Enter recipient, amount, and expense category. Upload a CSV for bulk disbursements.

02

Approver authorises with OTP

Your approver reviews and signs off with a 6-digit one-time code. No money moves without consent.

03

Funds land via M-Pesa instantly

Recipient receives funds in under 30 seconds, with automatic confirmation and audit record.

04

Categorise, reconcile, report

Every payment tagged to your chart of accounts. Export KRA-ready journals in one click.

FAQ

Questions we hear often

Straight answers about Centy, M-Pesa, and getting started. For anything else, use the contact form below or email hello@centycapital.com.

What is Centy?

Centy is one platform for finance operations (payments, payroll, expenses, books), people operations (roster, leave, time), and admin operations (documents, assets). It is built for teams in Kenya and East Africa, with M-Pesa-native workflows.

Does Centy support M-Pesa bulk payments?

Yes. Centy is designed around M-Pesa and bulk disbursements, with approval workflows and controls suited to finance teams paying suppliers, casual workers, and payroll.

Is Centy only for businesses in Kenya?

Centy is built first for Kenya and Kenyan compliance (KRA, statutory remittances). Teams elsewhere in East Africa use Centy where their workflows align — contact us to confirm fit for your country.

How do I get started?

Create an account on the Centy app to explore the product — no credit card required. You can also request a demo from the contact section on this page.

Do you offer demos or onboarding calls?

Yes. Submit the demo request form below and we will follow up to schedule a walkthrough tailored to your team.
