# 90-day SEO plan — Kenya market leadership

**Goal:** Build **unbeatable topical authority** on Kenyan payroll, bulk M-Pesa disbursements, casual workforce payments, and expense control—so Centy is the **default** answer in organic search for serious buyers.  

**Reality check:** SEO does not “annihilate” competitors overnight; Workpay-tier brands have years of links and awareness. This plan maximises **trajectory**: *faster crawl of trust, clearer intent match, fresher compliance story, harder-to-copy assets*. Judge success on **cluster growth** (impressions/clicks), **quality leads**, and **owned SERP share** on your pillars—not a single vanity rank on day 90.

**Prerequisites:** [`SEO-GOVERNANCE.md`](SEO-GOVERNANCE.md), [`SEO-KEYWORD-MAP.csv`](SEO-KEYWORD-MAP.csv). Update both as URLs go live.

## Operating cadence

- **Content:** ~**10–12 posts/month** (~**3/week**) on blog + **pillar maintenance**.
- **Review:** 30-min **Monday** GSC sweep; **month-end** governance report.
- **Compliance:** Any statutory post gets a **review owner** and **review date** in CSV.

## Phase 2 — Days 31–60: Scale content + moats

### Weeks 5–6 — Vertical & statutory depth

- Publish **2 vertical playbooks** (e.g. construction casuals; retail/shifts)—each links to casual + payroll pillars.
- Publish **2 statutory/ops** pieces (NSSF/SHIF/NITA refresh if needed; payroll mistakes SMEs).
- Start **compliance change log** category: short dated posts when rules shift (even 300 words + sources).

### Weeks 7–8 — Originality + distribution

- **Original mini-study** (survey or anonymised aggregate data)—only if methodology is honest. If not ready: **“Kenya SME payroll FAQ”** from real sales/support questions.
- **10 more** cluster articles (~3/week maintained).
- **Digital PR:** 3 pitches (journalist/podcast/accountant newsletter)—**one hook**, not a product dump.
- Refresh **weakest pillar section** using GSC: expand the H2 that gets impressions but page underperforms CTR.

**Phase 2 exit criteria:** ≥24 new purposeful URLs live YTD from day 1; **≥3 earned links** or equivalent brand mentions with URL; vertical pages ranking for long-tails.

---

## Phase 3 — Days 61–90: Consolidate, refresh, out-flank

### Weeks 9–10 — Consolidation

- **Internal linking pass:** Every post → pillar + 2 siblings; orphans = zero.
- **Cannibalisation check:** two URLs targeting same primary keyword → merge or differentiate primary intent.
- Publish **pillar refresh v1** (bulk M-Pesa or payroll—whichever drives most impressions).
- Publish **2 “vs / alternatives”** articles only if **accurate** and legal-reviewed (e.g. install vs cloud; API-first vs ops-first).

### Weeks 11–12 — Expansion & retargeting

- **8 more** articles focused on gaps from **GSC Queries** (you will have real data by now).
- **Second linkable asset:** interactive or highly printable (timeline, fee explainer with assumptions).
- **Video or Loom** summaries for top 3 pillars (YouTube + embed)—optional but strong for brand + long-tail.
- **Competitor SERP review:** who moved; **refresh titles/meta** on your pages with impression-rich, low-CTR queries.

**Phase 3 exit criteria:** All 4 pillars **refreshed or scheduled**; CSV keyword map **fully URL-populated** for shipped content; monthly reporting habit locked; clear “winning cluster” for Q2 doubling.

## Competitive positioning (Kenya) — how this plan pressures rivals

| They (typical) | You (this plan) |
|----------------|-----------------|
| Generic feature pages | **Pillars + verticals + compliance velocity** |
| Stale statutory text | **Dated updates + changelogs** |
| Thin “best of” lists | **One serious buyer guide + fair comparisons** |
| No linkable assets | **Checklists + data/FAQ hub** worth citing |
| Product-only | **Operator + (where true) technical narrative** |

You beat **WinguBox / Payroll.ke / Workpay** on SERPs by **owning Kenya-specific story depth and freshness** on a **tight cluster**—not by out-funding their brand everywhere.

## Risk register (don’t skip)

- Overclaiming tax/labour facts → **legal/compliance** review list in governance.
- Cheap paid links / PBNs → **prohibited**.
- Duplicate or AI-slop at scale → **quality bar** in publish checklist.
- Ignoring **message match** blog → landing → **bounce** kills inferred quality.
